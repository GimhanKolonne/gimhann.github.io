HIKKADUWA TURTLE HATCHERY

Welcome to the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery, where you can witness the incredible journey of sea turtles from eggs to the ocean. Our hatchery is dedicated to conserving and protecting these magnificent creatures.

At the hatchery, you can learn about the different species of sea turtles, their life cycles, and the conservation efforts being made to ensure their survival. We also offer educational programs, guided tours, and the opportunity to participate in releasing baby turtles into the sea.

The Turtle Farm in Hikkaduwa, also known as the Sea Turtle Hatchery & Rescue Center, is a small but very nice turtle farm and rescue center.
